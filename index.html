About Me

I'm an architectural designer, passionate about creating spaces that harmonize with their cultural and environmental context. My work explores the intersection of traditional Nepali architecture and contemporary design principles.

Design Philosophy

Creating spaces that tell stories, honor heritage, and embrace sustainable practices.

Specializations

Cultural spaces, sustainable design, vernacular architecture, and landscape integration.
